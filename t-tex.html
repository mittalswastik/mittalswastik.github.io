T-Tex

Timed Security in Multi-Threaded Real-Time Systems

Abstract

T-Tex introduces a timing-based intrusion-detection framework for OpenMP programs executing on heterogeneous multicore and GPU platforms. By instrumenting the OpenMP runtime, compiler, and kernel scheduler, T-Tex detects micro-architectural delay attacks as small as 60 ¬µs with 100 % accuracy while imposing < 2 % runtime overhead on representative cyber-physical workloads.
